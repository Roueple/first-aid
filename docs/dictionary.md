## 1. General Real Estate Terms

### Land & Property Classification

| Indonesian Term | English Term | Description |
|----------------|--------------|-------------|
| Tanah | Land | Raw land or plot |
| Kavling | Plot/Lot | Subdivided land ready for development |
| Lahan | Site/Land Area | General term for land area |
| Properti | Property | Real estate asset |
| Bangunan | Building | Physical structure |
| Aset Tetap | Fixed Asset | Immovable property |
| Aset Tidak Berwujud | Intangible Asset | Non-physical assets (goodwill, brand) |

### Land Rights (Hak Atas Tanah)

| Indonesian Term | English Term | Description | Duration |
|----------------|--------------|-------------|----------|
| HGB (Hak Guna Bangunan) | Right to Build | Right to construct on land | 30 years (extendable to 80) |
| HGU (Hak Guna Usaha) | Right to Cultivate | Commercial exploitation right | 35 years (extendable to 60) |
| Hak Milik | Freehold/Ownership | Full ownership (Indonesian citizens only) | Perpetual |
| Hak Pakai | Right to Use | Usage right for specific purpose | 25 years (extendable) |
| Hak Sewa | Leasehold | Rental/lease right | Per agreement |
| SHGB | HGB Certificate | Certificate of Right to Build | - |
| SHM | Freehold Certificate | Certificate of Ownership | - |
| SHMSRS | Strata Title Certificate | Apartment unit ownership | - |

### Area Measurements

| Indonesian Term | English Term | Description |
|----------------|--------------|-------------|
| Luas Tanah (LT) | Land Area | Total land size |
| Luas Bangunan (LB) | Building Area | Total built-up area |
| Luas Bruto | Gross Floor Area (GFA) | Total floor area including common areas |
| Luas Netto | Net Floor Area (NFA) | Usable floor area |
| Luas Semi Gross | Semi-Gross Area | Including partial common areas |
| KDB (Koefisien Dasar Bangunan) | Building Coverage Ratio (BCR) | Max building footprint % |
| KLB (Koefisien Lantai Bangunan) | Floor Area Ratio (FAR) | Max total floor area ratio |
| GSB (Garis Sempadan Bangunan) | Building Setback Line | Minimum distance from road |
| KDH (Koefisien Dasar Hijau) | Green Area Ratio | Minimum green space % |

### Development Phases

| Indonesian Term | English Term | Description |
|----------------|--------------|-------------|
| Pra-Pembangunan | Pre-Development | Planning & permitting phase |
| Pembangunan | Construction | Active construction phase |

- Land acquisition & title verification (HGB/SHM status)

- Construction progress vs. billing
- Marketing commission & agent agreements
- Customer installment collection & aging
- Building permit compliance (PBG/SLF)

- Strata title processing & handover timing
- Service charge collection & usage
- Sinking fund adequacy
- Building management contracts
- Common area maintenance costs
- PPPSRS compliance

- Room revenue reconciliation (PMS to GL)
- Occupancy verification (room inventory audit)
- F&B revenue & cost controls
- Banquet deposits & event billing
- Night audit procedures
- OTA commission & rebates
- Franchise/management fee calculations

- Patient revenue cycle (billing accuracy)
- BPJS claims & reimbursement
- Pharmaceutical inventory & dispensing
- Medical equipment maintenance
- Doctor fee arrangements
- Medical records confidentiality
- Licensing & accreditation compliance

### Clinic Audit Focus
- Service revenue recognition
- Inventory management (medical supplies)

- Licensing compliance (SIP, STR)
- Patient data privacy

### School Audit Focus
- Tuition fee collection & scholarships

- Accreditation compliance

### University Audit Focus
